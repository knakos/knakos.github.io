About Me

My career spans three decades in banking. I began as a financial systems analyst, moved into credit policy and risk management, then strategy and regulatory affairs, then large scale transformation. Today, I lead a systemic bank's HR Strategy & Organizational Effectiveness function.

Across my roles, I have found that great teams complemented with the right structures and systems turn strategy and vision into unprecedented outcomes. In fact, I am very proud to have also built and led such remarkable teams.

So, what's next? Let's raise the bar. Let's see what's possible when complementing our ingenious and imaginative selves with artificial intelligence.
